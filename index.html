Who are we?

Iron Claw is an First Robotics Competition (FRC) team run by the Los Gatos High School Robotics Club. This year we are restarting our FRC team at LGHS. We look forward to seeing our fellow teams at the Silicon Valley Regional in April 2016! We may also compete in the 2016 Central Valley Regional in March, depending on funding and the waitlist.
