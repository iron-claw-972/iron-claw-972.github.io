Who are we?

Iron Claw is a FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) team run by the Los Gatos High School Robotics Club. This is the team's second year at LGHS after being restarted. We look forward to seeing our fellow teams this year at the Central Valley Regional and at the Silicon Valley Regional!
